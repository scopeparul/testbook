About
Mel Robbins

Mel Robbins became one of the most trusted experts in the world on confidence and motivation the hard way: by first screwing up her own life.

That's why she's in a category all her own. As one of the most widely booked and followed podcast hosts and authors in the world, she's sought after by the world's leading brands and medical professionals for her research-backed tools and motivation. And, at the same time, Mel has amassed millions of followers online, with her advice going viral online almost daily.

A New York Times Bestselling author and self-publishing phenom, Mel’s work includes The High 5 Habit, The 5 Second Rule, and The Mel Robbins Podcast. Her female-led media company produces provocative, life-changing content, with millions of books sold, billions of video views, six #1 audiobooks, and one of the most viewed TEDx talks in the world. Her work has been translated into 41 languages and has changed the lives of millions of people worldwide.
