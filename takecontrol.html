About
Mel Robbins

Mel Robbins became one of the most trusted experts in change and motivation the hard way: by first screwing up her own life.

That’s why she’s in a category all her own. As one of the most widely booked and followed podcast hosts and authors in the world, she’s sought after by the world’s leading brands and medical professionals for her research-backed tools. At the same time, Mel has amassed millions of followers online, with her advice going viral online almost daily.
